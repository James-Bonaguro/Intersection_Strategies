What I Do

I help owners make clear decisions when the ground is shifting: pricing pressure, staffing, consolidation, competitor moves, and new tech that is hard to evaluate quickly.

My edge is that I have lived in both worlds: the day to day reality of a family business and the systems thinking of enterprise tech. I do not hand you a strategy deck. I help you see what is coming, pick a direction, and build a simple operating system that holds up.

Background

I grew up in Orland Park where my family owned Mercedes-Benz of Orland Park. I worked the sales floor, the finance office, and the service department. That is where you learn the real constraints: people, process, timing, and cashflow.

After a finance degree from Miami University and a stint in healthcare consulting, I came back to help run the family business. I focused on modernization: tightening operations, implementing finance and technology platforms, and building workflows that made work easier instead of harder.

Later I moved into enterprise technology, building internal tools and workflow systems used by 150+ people across multiple teams. That is where you learn what scales, what breaks, and how to build systems that survive growth.
