What I Do (In Plain English)

I help owners make better decisions when the ground is shifting—pricing pressure, new tech, competitor moves, staffing, consolidation, and changing customer expectations.

My edge is that I’ve lived in both worlds: the day-to-day reality of a family business and the systems thinking of enterprise tech. I don’t hand you a “strategy deck.” I help you see what’s coming, pick a direction, and build a simple operating system that holds up.

Why You Can Trust I’ll Get It

I grew up in Orland Park where my family owned Mercedes-Benz of Orland Park. I worked the sales floor, the finance office, and the service department. That’s where you learn the truth: a business doesn’t run on ideas—it runs on people, process, timing, and cashflow.

After a finance degree from Miami University and a stint in healthcare consulting, I came back to help run the family business. I focused on modernization: tightening operations, implementing finance/technology platforms, and building workflows that made work easier instead of harder.

Later I moved into enterprise technology—building internal tools and workflow systems used by 150+ people across multiple teams. That’s where you learn what scales, what breaks, and how to design systems that survive growth.

Today, Intersection Strategies is where those worlds meet: practical “owner brain” + enterprise-grade execution.
